## Use Case: "Cheat Sheet" for Engineering Leaders

### The Problem Leaders Face

Before engineering leadership meetings, ARB reviews, or engineering+product discussions:

1. **Need Multiple Perspectives:** One person can't see all angles
2. **Time Constraints:** Limited prep time before meetings
3. **Missing Context:** Don't always know what questions will come up
4. **Want to Impress:** Need to come prepared with thoughtful suggestions

### The Solution

**Input:** Situation/problem you're facing  
**Output:** Multi-perspective analysis with:
- Strategic recommendations (Director perspective)
- Team adoption plan (Manager perspective)
- Technical feasibility (Tech Lead perspective)
- Architecture considerations (Architect perspective)

**Result:** Leaders go into meetings prepared, confident, and ready to contribute.
