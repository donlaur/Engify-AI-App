**AI-First Organization Page:**

## Content Quality Standards

Based on research, content must:

1. **Be Strategic, Not Generic**
   - Move beyond "what is" to "how to" and "why"
   - Provide frameworks, not just definitions
   - Address C-suite concerns (ROI, risk, strategy)

2. **Include Hard Metrics**
   - Specific KPIs and measurement frameworks
   - Case studies with quantifiable results
   - ROI calculators and assessment tools

3. **Establish Authority**
   - Own emerging concepts (Verifiability, Context Engineering)
   - Provide unique frameworks not found elsewhere
   - Demonstrate deep technical and strategic expertise

4. **Be Actionable**
   - Step-by-step roadmaps
   - Comparison tables for decision-making
   - Downloadable templates and tools

5. **Address All Personas**
   - CTO: Strategic frameworks, ROI, governance
   - VP Engineering: Team structure, implementation
   - Product Director: Product org evolution, AI PM role
