## Content Generation Guidelines

### For Each Section:

1. **Start with the Problem** - Why does this matter to the target audience?
2. **Provide the Framework** - Clear, actionable structure
3. **Include Examples** - Real-world applications
4. **Add Metrics** - Quantifiable outcomes where possible
5. **Link to Related Content** - Internal linking opportunities
6. **End with Action Items** - What should the reader do next?

### Writing Style:

- **Strategic, not generic** - Address C-suite concerns
- **Data-driven** - Include metrics and research citations
- **Actionable** - Provide frameworks and tools
- **Authoritative** - Demonstrate deep expertise
- **Comprehensive** - Cover all aspects of the topic

### Visual Requirements:

- Comparison tables (AI-First vs AI-Native, KPIs, Team Structures)
- Framework diagrams (Roadmaps, Measurement frameworks)
- Process flows (Implementation phases)
- Infographics (Timelines, maturity models)
